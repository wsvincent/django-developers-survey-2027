# Django Developers Survey 2026

> *[internal] Document with ideas for this year Django Developers Survey 2026*

This official Django Developers Survey is the primary source of information about the Django community and helps guide future development.

It should take you about 10 minutes to complete.

After the survey is over, we will publish the aggregated results and randomly choose 10 winners (from those who complete the survey in its entirety with meaningful answers), who will each receive a USD 100 Amazon Gift Card or a local currency equivalent.

We are running the survey from April 15 to May 31, 2026. The results and infographics will be published in mid-August.

Thank you for taking the time to contribute to this community effort!

The Django Software Foundation and JetBrains

This survey is organized in partnership with the Django Software Foundation and JetBrains. It's hosted by JetBrains, so your responses will be subject to the JetBrains General Market Research Terms and Conditions.
